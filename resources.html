Organized by: Eigerlab

EIGERlab established the Information Technology (IT) Roundtable and Code for Rockford in order to:

  • develop and market a brand which establishes the Rockford region as a thriving IT business community to
  • attract more professionals, start-ups and existing businesses to the community;
  • establish a strong connected community of IT professionals within the Rockford region;
  • facilitate resources which assist IT employers in overcoming barriers to growth; and
  • develop a passionate group of civic leaders and technologists focused on better connecting government to citizens through OpenData and technology.
